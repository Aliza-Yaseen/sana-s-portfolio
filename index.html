About Me

I’m Sana Riaz, a passionate fourth-year law student at Shaheed Zulfiqar Ali Bhutto University of Law, Karachi. With a deep commitment to human rights and social justice, I’ve dedicated my academic journey to understanding and advocating for those who need a voice.

My experiences, from leading initiatives in the Women Empowerment Cell to engaging in critical legal research, have shaped me into a thoughtful and driven advocate. I believe in the power of law to transform lives, and I’m committed to using my skills in legal writing, public speaking, and conflict resolution to make a positive impact.

Fluent in English and Urdu, I aim to bridge gaps and build understanding through my work. As I continue my journey in law, my focus remains on defending rights and shaping a more just and equitable world.
